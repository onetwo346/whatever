Air Cargo

Our air cargo service provides fast and reliable international delivery options. With our extensive network of airlines, we ensure quick and secure transport of your packages. Average delivery times range from 1 to 5 days, depending on the destination.

Ideal for urgent and time-sensitive shipments, air cargo is a premium option that guarantees swift delivery. We offer real-time tracking, so you can monitor your shipment's progress from departure to arrival.

Advantages: Fastest delivery, high reliability, suitable for high-value items, and excellent tracking capabilities.

Disadvantages: Higher cost compared to other shipping methods, limited capacity for oversized items.

Recommended For: Expedited shipments, international deliveries, and high-priority cargo.

Sea Cargo

Sea cargo is the most cost-effective method for transporting large and bulky shipments. We provide efficient maritime transportation with an average delivery time of 20 to 40 days, depending on the route and destination.

Perfect for non-urgent and bulk shipments, sea cargo offers significant cost savings for large quantities of goods. We ensure safe and secure loading, transport, and unloading of your cargo.

Advantages: Economical for large volumes, suitable for oversized items, and less time-sensitive.

Disadvantages: Longer transit times, potential for weather-related delays, and less frequent updates.

Recommended For: Bulk shipments, non-urgent deliveries, and international trade.

Truck Cargo

Our trucking service provides flexible and timely delivery options for local and regional shipments. With an average delivery time of 1 to 7 days, our trucks offer reliable transport for a variety of cargo types.

Ideal for shipments within the same country or neighboring regions, our truck cargo service ensures safe and efficient transport with customizable delivery schedules.

Advantages: Flexible scheduling, suitable for local and regional deliveries, and cost-effective for short distances.

Disadvantages: Limited to road transport, potential for delays due to traffic or weather.

Recommended For: Local deliveries, scheduled shipments, and regional transport.
